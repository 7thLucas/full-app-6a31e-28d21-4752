The Problem

Arabic language teachers at the preparatory secondary level spend an estimated 2–3 hours per week manually browsing scattered internet sources for reading texts. No dedicated tool exists that filters texts by type and reading level for الثانوي الإعدادي — leaving educators to sift through irrelevant, off-level results with no efficient alternative.

The Solution

نصوص aggregates Arabic reading texts from across the internet and presents them filtered by text type (narrative, descriptive, argumentative, expository, literary) and calibrated to the preparatory secondary level — so teachers find a lesson-ready text in under 60 seconds.
